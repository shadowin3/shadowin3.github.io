This Site is a List of Echo's Commands, Sorted By The Type of Command

Echo is designed to pick up on commands by finding keywords within the message. He will not, however, carry out a command unless called upon.

To call upon Echo, you can use practically any manner of addressing him, be it by saying his name, mentioning him, or even delivering the command within a private message to Echo.

If you are in a rush, you may choose to call upon him by including a backslash (/) followed by the letter E, and then using any of the keywords of his various commands.
