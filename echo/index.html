Welcome To Echo's Official Website!

Here you can find documentation on all of Echo's commands, which is kept up to date with each command added. The tabs on the navigation bar are meant to simplify the search for commands by classifying them by the type of action they perform.

Usage

Echo's commands can be used in a variety of ways. As long as one of the command's keywords (listed on this site) are used and Echo is either addressed directly or the message is sent via Private Message, he will perform the action involved with that command. Additionally, for quicker use, Echo can be addressed with the use of his prefix, a backslash followed by the letter 'E,' should you have the need for it. (/e)

Why Use Echo?

Unlike most other bots for Discord, Echo takes no specific specialization; He is a multipurpose bot, and the majority of his content comes from community suggestions and requests. This means that he is not just a music or video bot, but rather performs a plethora of random functions. He can help in finding youtube videos, search Google for some top results, send a selection of images, memes, and gifs, and perform a variety of text-based chat games, involving rock-paper-scissors, 8 ball, and much more.

Contributing

Because Echo is built around and created for the community, we accept every little bit of contribution that you offer. If you have any suggestions, wish to request that a command be added, or just have a question, feel free to contact us and share in Echo's Discord Server. If Discord is not your style, there are many other ways to get ahold of Azzy directly. We can't wait to hear from you!

Echo Bot Info

Echo was coded by Azanoxious in JavaScript along with the help of SplitPixl, and is intended to aid in the use of the discord client and make day-to-day life on the internet easier.
